About Leadership Foundation

Leadership Foundation embarked on a journey to transform engineering education in India. The key challenge to be addressed was the dismally low employability and entrepreneurship skills of engineering graduates in the country.

Leadership Foundation sought to create a quantum improvement in the situation by bringing about systematic change in the existing learning paradigms in engineering education. The leadership goals were ambitious. Yet, such social initiatives are judged not on intent but on impact. Leadership Foundation has teamed up with academia with the aims of enhancing Research, Employability and Entrepreneurship skills of graduate engineers in India.

Today, Leadership Foundation are over several Engineering colleges .This is changing the way Engineering is being taught and learnt in the country.
